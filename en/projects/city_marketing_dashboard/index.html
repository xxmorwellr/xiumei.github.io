City Marketing Dashboard

This is my end-to-end developed data product during my first full-time job, aiming to guide toB & toC marketing for our novel new energy brand Weltmeister. It had been delivered successfully and worked well.

1. Product Description#

Completed work:

  1. Construction of city ranking system, you can see the overall ranking: top10, top30, top50
  2. Complete data aggregation and indicator system construction for 306 cities
  3. With the city panel display tool, you can observe the performance of each city on specific indicator factors and specific factor items, including:
  • Overall auto market;
  • Brand potential;
  • Basic urban development;
  • Traffic and purchase restriction policies;
  • Dealer channels

Follow-up:

  1. For the existing toC city marketing panel, use the final data results to form a feedback mechanism to increase the richness of indicators and the usability of tools.
  2. An evaluation system for toB’s overall sales potential from a key customer perspective.
  3. With sales execution as the goal, refine the direct help of the data results in front of sales execution, including:
    a) Assessment of user concentration at the community level and assessment of the completeness of basic charging facilities;
    b) Sorting out the global new energy vehicle owner labeling system to answer who the new energy users are in different cities and what kind of cars they need;
    c) Key customer failure analysis tool

2. Open Tool#

This tool uses a browser as the display and analysis platform, and requires the use of Chrome/Safari/Opera/Firefox/IE (>= 11.0) and other browsers that support HTML5. Since the displayed platform will need to load online map resources in the future, it needs to be browsed on a PC with a network environment. It is recommended to use a resolution >1024*768.
